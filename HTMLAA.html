We are a New York based automotive detailing service. Automotive Allure specializes in the preservation and restoration of automotive paint, as well as interior materials; vinyl, leather, etc. Day-to-day use and exposure to natural elements results in oxidation and wear. Our services are used to eliminate such, and not only resurrect the experience of a showroom quality vehicle, but ensure that it lasts as long as possible. Keeping one’s personal vehicle clean has many benefits; most importantly, it will allow the owner and mechanics to identify problems much easier. Our services range in price due to various factors, and we cater towards the worker/enthusiast who is not willing to risk the damages imminent from an automated car wash. Automotive Allure is run by car-enthusiasts with experience in the field; and while we are not limited to the services listed, we are consistently challenging ourselves in both our work and towards innovation.

The Basic:
The most economical service available. Includes an interior and exterior wash plus a light vacuum. Finished off with wheel and paint glaze.
The Sunday Routine:
This service consists of an interior and exterior wash, with a vacuum. A liquid wax is applied to the paint for shine as well as add a layer of protection.
The Claybar:
A claybar is a service that improves the feel of your vehicle’s paint. Throughout its life, dirt becomes embedded into its surface, and can cause the paint to become coarse and murky. A clay material is applied to the paint, removing embedded particles. Bringing back a glassy smooth finish. In addition to this, a stronger, paste wax-sealant is applied for long term durability and protection of paint.
The Compound:
The compound service is meant to truly bring out the shine in a vehicles paint. The panels on a vehicle come into contact with many items throughout it’s life, and they cause microscopic, shallow scratches in upper layer of paint. These, swirl marks, and oxidation of the paint under sun exposure, can blemish the paint and remove it’s factory shine. The Compound removes the top layer of contaminated, oxidized paint, where it is replaced with a coat of sealant-wax.
The Works:
The works is the most drastic service available. It includes all services listen above, including a wash, claybar, compound, and wax.
